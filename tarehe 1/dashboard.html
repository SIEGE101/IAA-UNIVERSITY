About Us

The Institute of Accountancy Arusha (IAA) aims to provide high-quality education and training in accounting, finance, and management disciplines. Our mission is to produce well-rounded professionals equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the dynamic global market.

Our Courses

Bachelor of Accounting

Comprehensive training in accounting principles, financial reporting, and audit practices.

Master of Business Administration

Advanced management and leadership skills for aspiring business professionals.

Diploma in Procurement and Supply

Specialized knowledge in procurement processes, supply chain management, and logistics.

Bachelor of Computer Science

In-depth understanding of computer science concepts, programming, and system design.

Diploma in Computer and Information Technology

Fundamental training in IT skills, software development, and information systems.

Bachelor of Finance

Focused education on financial management, investment strategies, and market analysis.
